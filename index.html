Vancouver - Keiretsu Forum

The Keiretsu Forum Northwest region formed in 2005 and is comprised of the Boise, Kirkland-Eastside, Portland, Seattle, Spokane-Inland, Tacoma-South Puget Sound, and Vancouver chapters. The Vancouver chapter launched in September 2012 and enjoys close synergies with the regional and global Keiretsu Forum angel investment network.
