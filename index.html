San Francisco - Keiretsu Forum

The Keiretsu Forum Northern California region formed in 2000 and is comprised of the East Bay, San Francisco/North Bay, and Silicon Valley chapters. The San Francisco chapter was launched in September 2005 and enjoys close synergies with the regional and global Keiretsu Forum angel investment network. The Northern California region has 300 members.
